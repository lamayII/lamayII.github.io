Our Rich History

Established months after the end of the Mexican-American War in 1851, the Santa Modesta Republic rose to become one of the most developed and thriving states in America following a Gold Rush that swept the state. Due to the territory’s geographical diversity, and rich resources, the state was soon to be known as “The Golden State” by all who resided in the Pacific Coast.
